Our History

Mbagi Ltd. was founded in the year 1978 by Edward Kariuki Kimani & Mary Kanyi Kimani with the vision of transforming the real estate industry by providing top-tier residential and commercial spaces. With years of experience, we have established ourselves as a trusted name in real estate, delivering exceptional properties and services that exceed our clients' expectations.

Since our inception, we have consistently grown our portfolio, expanded our services, and built lasting relationships with our clients and partners.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the leading real estate company known for quality, trust, and innovation. We aim to set new standards in the industry and create lasting value for our clients and communities.
